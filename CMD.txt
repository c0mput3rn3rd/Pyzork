PickUp [item]: Pick up an item that is in the room
Go [direction(North,East,South,West)]: move in a direction
Destroy [item]: Destroys that item in your inventory
Show [(Map or Inventory)]: displays a map (if you have one) or the contents of your inventory
Search: Searches the room you are in, revealing items
Use [Cellphone]: 20% chance of rescue, only one use
Help: Displays this menu
